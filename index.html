About Us

Atlas Foods Venture is a leading food manufacturing company based in Karu, Nigeria. Located at Monday Market, New Karu, we specialize in producing high-quality food products that meet international standards while maintaining affordability for the Nigerian market. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern manufacturing techniques with strict quality control measures to ensure every product that leaves our doors is safe, nutritious, and delicious. We are committed to contributing to Nigeria's food security and economic growth through local production and job creation.
