About Us

Blessed Lily Restaurant Tech Limited (also known as Blessed Lily Ghost Kitchen Co) is a forward-thinking FoodTech company based in Monday Market, New Karu. Founded by a passionate plant-based foods entrepreneur, we're dedicated to transforming how Nigerians experience healthy, sustainable cuisine. Our ghost kitchen model allows us to deliver restaurant-quality plant-based meals efficiently and affordably, making wholesome eating accessible to everyone in our community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a ghost kitchen?

A ghost kitchen is a professional food preparation facility optimized for delivery and takeout orders. We don't have a traditional dine-in restaurant, which allows us to focus entirely on food quality and efficient delivery.

Are your meals 100% plant-based?

Yes! All our meals are crafted using plant-based ingredients, making them perfect for vegetarians, vegans, and anyone looking to incorporate more plant-based foods into their diet.

Do you deliver outside Karu?

Currently, we primarily serve the Karu area, but we're expanding our delivery radius. Contact us to check if we deliver to your location.

How do I place an order?

Simply call or WhatsApp us at 08176801759. Our team will guide you through our menu options and arrange delivery to your location.

Can I customize my meals?

Absolutely! We're happy to accommodate dietary preferences and restrictions. Just let us know your requirements when ordering.
